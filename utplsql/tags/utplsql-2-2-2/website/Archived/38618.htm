Licencing of utPLSQL Chris Rimmer chrisrimmer chris.rimmer@tessella.co.uk Yes, I've taken a look and the GPL is drawn up so that you can bundle it with any software without alteration. You just need to refer to it in the source.

The text of the GPL is at http://www.gnu.org/copyleft/gpl.txt - it actually includes a description of how to apply it to your own software (see the section at the end).

Basically, it allows the free modification and distribution of the software. It also ensures that any such distribution continues to be under the terms of the GPL.
